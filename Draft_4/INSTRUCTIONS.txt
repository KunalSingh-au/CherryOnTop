MOST IMPORTANT TASK BEFORE SATURDAY
====================================
You need to add two columns to data/qa_gold/gold_qa.csv:

  gold_answer_en  — the correct English answer (copy from PDF)
  keywords_en     — 2-3 keywords separated by | that MUST be in correct answer

Without gold_answer_en, the evaluation cannot run.

HOW TO DO IT
────────────
1. Open gold_qa.csv in Excel or Google Sheets
2. Add two new columns: gold_answer_en, keywords_en
3. For each question, open the corresponding PDF
4. Find the answer and copy the key fact as gold_answer_en
5. Write 2-3 keywords as keywords_en

EXAMPLE
───────
question_hi: AYURGYAN योजना के अंतर्गत कुल कितने Research & Innovation प्रोजेक्ट स्वीकृत किए गए?
gold_answer_en: 147 Research and Innovation projects have been approved
keywords_en: 147|one hundred forty seven

question_hi: NAM के तहत कितने बिस्तरों वाले एकीकृत आयुष अस्पताल स्थापित किए जा सकते हैं?
gold_answer_en: Integrated AYUSH hospitals with 10, 30 or 50 beds
keywords_en: 10|30|50

GOOD ANSWERS (specific, verifiable)
  Numbers:    "Nine national institutes"
  Names:      "NIEPVD, Dehradun"
  Yes/No:     "No, this is the responsibility of State governments"
  Amounts:    "Rs. 4315.15 crore"

BAD ANSWERS (too vague to evaluate)
  "The government has taken various steps"
  "As per the document"

KEYWORDS TIPS
  Use | to separate alternatives
  Include the number AND the word: "nine|9|नौ"
  For names: "NIEPVD|National Institute for Visual"
  For yes/no: "yes|हाँ" or "no|नहीं|state government"

Time estimate: ~2-3 hours for all 100 questions.
Do this Friday evening so Saturday you just run the code.
